Welcome to the Lost & Found System

Important Notice

This Lost & Found system is exclusively for MMU students and staff. Access and reporting are limited to authorized campus members only. If you are not part of MMU or cannot find your lost item in this system, please visit the campus Lost & Found office directly for assistance.

This platform is designed to help students and staff easily report, find, and track lost and found items across the campus. Our goal is to reunite lost belongings with their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.

How to Use the System

1. To get started, Sign Up or if you already have an account, Log In to access all features.

2. If you have lost an item, use the Add Item page to report it. Be sure to select “Lost” as the status and provide detailed descriptions and location by clicking on the map.

3. Found something? Report it by going to the same Add Item page, choose “Found” as the status, and fill in details about the item and location.

4.To look for lost or found items, visit the Browse page where you can search or filter items by date, status, or keywords.

5. Once you find a matching item, contact the reporter directly via the Chat feature to arrange pickup or ask questions.

6. Once you've used the service, share your experience through the Submit Feedback feature to help us improve.

7. After retrieving or returning an item, please update its status to Returned to keep the system up-to-date for all users.

Important Tips if You Lose Something

  • Report your lost item here as soon as possible to increase chances of recovery.
  • Stop or temporarily freeze any lost credit/debit cards immediately by contacting your bank.
  • Change passwords for any online accounts if your phone, laptop, or other devices are lost.
  • Keep an eye on your email or chat messages for any updates or found items matching your description.
  • Visit the campus Lost & Found office if needed for in-person assistance.
  • Always provide accurate and detailed descriptions to help others identify your item.

Thank You for Helping Keep Our Campus Safe

By using this system, you’re helping create a more secure and connected community. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact our support team.
