Welcome to the Lost & Found System

This platform is designed to help students and staff easily report, find, and track lost and found items across the campus.

How to Use the System

  1. Create an account: Please sign up or log in if you already have one.
  2. Report a Lost Item: If you lose something, add a lost item report describing your item with as much detail as possible (color, brand, unique marks).
  3. Report a Found Item: If you find an item, please add a found item report to help reunite it with its owner.
  4. Browse Items: Check the browse page regularly to see if your lost item has been found.
  5. Contact: Use the chat feature to communicate with the person who reported the item.

Important Tips if You Lose Something

  • Report your lost item here as soon as possible.
  • Stop or temporarily freeze any lost credit/debit cards immediately by contacting your bank.
  • Change passwords for any online accounts if your phone or laptop is lost.
  • Keep an eye on your email or chat messages for any updates or found items matching your description.
  • Visit the campus Lost & Found office if needed.

Thank You for Helping Keep Our Campus Safe

By using this system, you’re helping create a more secure and connected community. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact our support team.
